Souvlaki

Description

Souvlaki is a popular Greek fast food consisting of small pieces of meat and sometimes vegetables grilled on a skewer. It is commonly served with pita bread, garnished with tomato, onion, and tzatziki sauce.

This dish is beloved across Greece and represents simple, delicious, and traditional Greek street food.

Ingredients

  • 500g pork, chicken, or lamb, cut into small cubes
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Pita bread, sliced tomatoes, onions, and tzatziki for serving
  • Wooden or metal skewers

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the meat cubes and marinate for at least 1 hour in the refrigerator.
  3. Thread the meat cubes onto skewers.
  4. Preheat the grill or grill pan to medium-high heat.
  5. Grill the skewers for 8-10 minutes, turning occasionally, until cooked through and slightly charred.
  6. Warm the pita bread and assemble with grilled meat, tomatoes, onions, and tzatziki.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!
