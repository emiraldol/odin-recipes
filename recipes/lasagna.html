Description

sagna is a classic Italian dish known for its layers of flat pasta, rich sauces, and melted cheese. It typically consists of alternating layers of wide pasta sheets, meat or vegetable fillings, and a combination of tomato sauce and béchamel (a creamy white sauce). The entire creation is then baked until hot and bubbly, with the cheese melted and browned.

Ingredients

  • 2 olive oil. plus extra for the dish.
  • 750g lean beef mince.
  • 90g pack prosciutto.
  • 800g passata or half our basic tomato sauce.

Steps

  1. Heat the oven to 400ºF.
  2. Brown the beef and onion.
  3. Prepare the baking dish and assemble the meat sauce.
  4. Begin layering the lasagna.
